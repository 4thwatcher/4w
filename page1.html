Endtime of the age

The Gospel of the Kingdom (How it will be at the end of this age).

Introduction

A child of a president has no guarantee to one day be a president or to have a position in
the White House.
However, the child of a King, by birthright has the promise of royalty and of inheritance
in the Kingdom. A child of a King, can EXPECT to one day wear a crown, and to have
rulership.

Responsive Design

Connect the dots and it makes SENSE, why the scripture says that in Christ you are
children of the King and that at his Christ's return you will literally "rule over over
nations" on this earth (Revelation 2:26) (Revelation 20:4), and that you will wear a crown
(James 1:12)!   Remember, the scripture teaches that when Jesus returns there will be a
glorious period of 1000 years (messianic millennial reign) when he sets up his Kingdom
on THIS earth (Zechariah 14:9, Revelation 20:6).   So When he returns, he will set up
his throne to rule on earth just as the messianic prophecies state (2 Samuel 7:16; Daniel
7:22).   The book of Zachariah 14:16 says that when Jesus returns as King over the
earth, he will put a stop to the war against Jerusalem, and that there will be survivors
from the nations who will begin to worship him. Many people from these nations will
become citizens of his rulership. However, those returning to the earth WITH Jesus at his
return will be his called, his chosen, his immortal royal priesthood, 'YOU'.   You, will
RULE alongside Christ over the remaining nations. For those who have followed Christ
in this age, and have called him Lord, will be fellow Kings WITH Christ in the millennial
reign age (Romans 8:17).  -The promises of God are great! This goes all the way back
to the promises of God given to the seed of Abraham in the Old Testament, for it was
prophesied that the people of God would be Kings and priests over the entire world
(Exodus 19:6).   It gets deeper.

Smart Watch

Now we know an heir cannot rule or take up their inheritance until the King dies. And
our Father in heaven is a KING who cannot die. HOWEVER, in his great wisdom, our
Father in heaven, the King, took on the form of a mortal man through Jesus, and died on
the cross, so that we could legally receive the inheritance of his Kingdom. And in his
great power, he did not remain in death, but raised his body (Jesus) to life again, to Rule
as the King of kings.
